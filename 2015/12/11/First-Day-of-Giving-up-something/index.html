First Day of Giving up something

Posted on 2015-12-11   |   In Diary

Put some other things aside

I downloaded agent of shield, flash and NBA games but didn’t watch them today, instead I calm down to do leetcode exercises and work out int the nyu gym, what a day, and I finally organize why I am working so hard.
GF I want meet is like the life I want to live, now it is beyond my ability, I always thought I can meet someone who is beautiful, kind and can understand me, the kind of girls are there, but why they will choose me, am I the same handsome, kind, wealthy and have all kinds of abilities? No, I am not now. So I will meet the girls who can not be up to my expectation, or who will not consider about me.
It is like the life I want to live. One is beyond my ability, the other is not my expectation.
I knew there are two ways a long time ago. On the one hand, lower my expectations, will meet the girl or live the life in the range I can touch, but that is so hard, so hard, so hard fro me.
On the other hand, raise my ability. It seems now to be the only way I can think about and to go. It needs my heart to be calm, confident and focus, it needs time to accumulate, it needs patience, it needs money, it needs to take a lot to practice many abilities, it needs fortune, it needs challenge for lots of difficulty. It is still easy than the first one way, and I am doing it now.
So no more happier things than getting on my way.
